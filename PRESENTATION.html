Today's Pain Points

  • Business users depend on data analysts to write every SQL query — creating a queue
  • Reports take hours or days; by the time they arrive, the moment has passed
  • Non-technical staff cannot explore data independently — insight stays siloed
  • Compliance and fraud insights require specialist skills that are scarce and expensive

With the AI Suite

  • Any business user types a question in plain English and receives a structured answer instantly
  • The AI writes and executes safe, validated SQL on its own — no analyst required
  • Every answer is traceable: the SQL used, the data returned, and the reasoning recorded
  • Quality guardrails prevent dangerous or inaccurate responses from reaching users
